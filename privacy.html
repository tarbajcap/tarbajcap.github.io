CAP Privacy Policy

Operator: Kamil Nalevanko, Slovakia

1. Introduction

We respect your privacy. CAP does not require user registration and does not intentionally collect personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

Certain technical information may be processed by third-party services used within the application, such as advertising providers and AI services, solely to provide app functionality.

2. Information We Process

Advertising (Google AdMob)

CAP displays advertisements through Google AdMob.

Google AdMob may process certain information, including:

  • Advertising ID (GAID)
  • IP address
  • Device type and operating system version
  • Language settings
  • Advertising interactions (ad views and clicks)
  • Approximate location derived from IP address

For users located in the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, and Switzerland, CAP displays a consent form in accordance with GDPR and applicable privacy regulations. Personalized advertising is shown only when consent has been granted.

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

AI Assistant (Google Gemini)

When using the AI Assistant feature, the text you enter may be sent to Google's Gemini API to generate a response.

Only the content you voluntarily submit is processed. CAP does not attach your name, email address, or other identifying information to these requests.

Gemini Terms and Policies: https://ai.google.dev/terms

Backend Services (Supabase)

CAP uses Supabase to deliver current product prices, flyer information, and application data.

Requests may include technical information necessary to provide the service, but CAP does not intentionally collect personal user information through these requests.

Local Storage

The application stores certain preferences locally on your device, including:

  • Selected language
  • Selected country
  • Favorite products
  • Shopping lists
  • Saved AI-generated recipes

This information remains on your device and is not transmitted to CAP servers.

CAP does not store personal user data on its own servers. Local application data remains on the user's device until deleted by the user or by uninstalling the application.

3. Information We Do Not Collect

CAP does not intentionally collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Precise GPS location
  • Contacts
  • Photos
  • Files stored on your device

CAP does not use custom analytics tools for user profiling.

4. Children's Privacy

CAP is not intended for children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

5. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Union, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of data
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • Withdraw consent for personalized advertising
  • Lodge a complaint with your local Data Protection Authority

You may manage advertising consent directly within the application settings.
